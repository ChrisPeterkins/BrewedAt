Racing

Mario Kart

Nintendo Switch & Switch 2

BrewedAt's Mario Kart 8 Hall of Champions — where speed, strategy, and a little bit of beer-fueled chaos collide.

Every race is battled out in 150cc on the Nintendo Switch, with racers overcoming 64 competitors across intense rounds of elimination. Only one can claim the crown, making each champion's journey to the top a testament to skill, adaptability, and maybe a little luck.

Each champion earns their place in the Hall of Champions — a growing legacy of BrewedAt's best. Bragging rights are eternal, but the journey to the top is never easy.

Fighting

Super Smash Bros. Ultimate

Nintendo Switch

BrewedAt's Super Smash Hall of Champions — where reflexes, mind games, and a touch of beer-fueled hype come together in glorious battle.

Every match is fought on the Nintendo Switch in classic 4 player mosh, 3-stock, no items. Players clash through a gauntlet of 64 contenders in a high-stakes, bracket-style showdown. Only one emerges victorious, earning their place among legends.

Each champion earns their rightful spot in the Hall of Champions — a living legacy of BrewedAt's finest fighters. Bragging rights last forever, but claiming the title takes nerves of steel, precise reads, and maybe just one clutch recovery.
