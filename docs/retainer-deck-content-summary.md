### 3. Crafted In Philly & Jersey
**Description:** 3-month digital brewery pass event series

**How It Works:**
- Ticket holders receive one free glass of beer at participating local taprooms
- Community votes to crown their favorites (atmosphere, beer, etc.)
- Uses Let's Rallie app for check-ins and voting

**2025 Metrics (Philly):**
- 207 tickets sold (57% increase over 2024)
- 1,254 total visits driven (656 increase over 2024)
- 581 first-time visits (252 increase over 2024)
- 85% Gen Z or Millennials

**Timeline:**
- Crafted In Philly: April 25th - July 31st
- Crafted In Jersey: June 1st - August 31st

**Press Coverage:** Featured in local media
